Camping information.

Primitive Camping

We offer primitive camping onsite to anyone that would like to stay for the weekend. Camping is open from Friday June 1st to Sunday June 3rd. There are no water/sewer hookups or fires allowed aside from the main bonfire on the property.

Campgrounds

For anyone that would like to camp in something other than a tent (RV, popup, camper, etc.) there are several campgrounds located right next to the property.
Hickory Lake Campground | Shady Acres Campground
